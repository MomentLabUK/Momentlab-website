Our Booths

The Studio

Our signature open-air booth.

Crafted from natural wood. Built around a professional DSLR. Designed to make every guest look their best.

Enquire About The Studio

Photobooth quality, reimagined.

Most photobooths settle for "good enough." The Studio doesn't. Behind its warm wooden frame sits a professional DSLR camera and a beauty dish light — the same kit used by portrait photographers. Guests step up, the light does the work, and the result is a beautifully lit, professional-grade photo, printed in seconds and shared instantly.

It's the kind of booth people queue for. And the kind of photo they actually keep.

Why The Studio

Designed with Every Detail in Mind.

Feature · 01

Dual-Screen Display

Front screen for live previews, back screen for the moments your event deserves — custom branding, engagement photos, a live gallery, yours to shape.

Feature · 02

Beauty Dish Lighting

Every guest, perfectly lit. Crisp, flattering 24.1-megapixel photos that hold up at any size.

Feature · 03

Instant Digital Sharing

AirDrop, text, or email. Your guests' photos are on their phones before they've left the booth.

Feature · 04

Built-In Marshall Speaker

No DJ? No problem. Premium sound built in — for when the booth becomes the party.

05

A Booth That Belongs in the Room

Hand-crafted from natural wood with a warm, timeless finish — The Studio blends into any venue without overpowering the space. No bulky plastic. No distracting branding.

06

Open-Air Design

No curtains, no cramped boxes. The open-air setup means groups of any size can jump in together — and it keeps the energy around the booth high all night.

07

Prints in Seconds

Photos printed on-site in seconds using professional dye-sublimation technology. Classic strip (2×6) or postcard (4×6) — both on premium, smudge-proof paper built to last.
